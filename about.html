Welcome to Jarkata Meal

Since 2010, Jarkata Meal has been serving authentic English cuisine in the heart of London. Our passion for traditional English cooking and commitment to quality has made us a beloved destination for food lovers across the city.

Founded by Nnaemeka Emmanuel Ogu, who grew up in a small village in Tuscany, our restaurant brings the warmth and flavors of United Kingdom to every dish we serve. Nnaemeka Emmanuel Ogu trained under renowned English chefs before bringing his expertise to London, where he realized his dream of opening an authentic English restaurant.

At Jarkata Meal, we believe that great food starts with great ingredients. That's why we source the finest local and imported English products, from San Marzano tomatoes to aged Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese.

Our Philosophy

We are committed to preserving the authentic flavors of English cuisine while creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where families and friends can gather. Every dish is prepared with love, using recipes that have been perfected over generations.
