Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a reservation?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially for dinner and weekends, to ensure we have a table available for you.

What is your cancellation policy?

We kindly ask that you cancel or modify your reservation at least 2 hours in advance. You can call us or reply to your confirmation email.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes! We can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free diets. Please inform us of any allergies or restrictions when making your reservation.

Is there a dress code?

We have a smart casual dress code. While we don't require formal attire, we ask that guests avoid beachwear, athletic wear, and overly casual clothing.

Do you offer private dining?

Yes! We have several private dining spaces available for events. Visit our Events page for more information.

Can I bring my own wine?

We have an extensive wine list, but we do allow BYOB with a ¬£25 corkage fee per bottle. Limit of 2 bottles per table.
